About Us

With over 100,000 registered students, Eduwizards is the world's favorite tutoring website.
We are launching tutoring for India in April 2015 and are starting with Home Tutoring for Delhi /NCR students only.
In the coming months we will open Home Tutoring option for students in all cities and towns of India.
Online Tutoring, of course, does not depend on student's location and will be available to all students starting April 2015.

Eduwizards features India's best home and online tutors, ranked and reviewed by students. We aim to openly identify India's best tutors, and have them constantly prove their excellence by getting top reviews from students.

At our website you can view tutor details and select the tutor you want OR our staff can find the right tutor for you!

Eduwizards was founded in India in 2007 and is now a multinational corporation, with offices in India and USA.

We have been providing online tutoring within the USA (and globally) since 2007. We are now expanding to provide tutoring (both Face-to-face Home Tutoring and Online Tutoring) to students in India.

We also provide an excellent opportunity for India's top tutors. There are no charges for tutors to sign up, have their submitted profile approved and be listed as a tutor on our website. We do not charge tutors until they get paid by students for tutoring sessions. Our basic model is to keep a percentage of the tutor's earnings as a "transaction fee."

We also have the option of Online Tutoring available for Indian Tutors who have broadband internet with power backup. Tutors who, in addition to these two requirements, have Excellent English with Neutral Accent can also give Online Tutoring directly to USA and other International students and earn in U.S. Dollars.
